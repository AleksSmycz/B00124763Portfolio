Story behind the Warsaw-pact Cookbook

The Warsaw-pact cookbook was made as a college project. It is a collection of eastern European recipes you would typically find in the Soviet Union and its, satellite states.The Warsaw pact cookbook was very interesting project for me, it was a college project but I had a lot of fun with it. It had to follow the constructivism art style which was perfect for the communist propaganda style was going for.

I was always interested in the communist propaganda art style as I am from Poland which is a previous Soviet Union socialist state, this meant I really enjoyed making each design for this book and even gathering the recipes as I tend to have a lot of eastern European friends I simply asked them what was a popular dish in their country during those hard times. I think it works well as a cookbook combining both cooking and constructivism was a great idea and I believe I executed it well.

Why make Blender assets

I made these blender assets for a unity game me and my friends where working on for a college module called group project. I made these to learn blender and I needed to make them as key assets we didnâ€™t already have in the game.

Story behind the Blender assets

The assets are in a low poly art style as this was the chosen one for the game. I started off by looking at several tutorials on how to model assets in blender. This was followed by looking how to model assets in low poly art style. Several key assets where then made the most important being the pill bottle and glass bottle. This was followed by the trees which I tried to keep diverse so that the person placing them in unity had choices on how he or she wants to model the area.

Creating blender assets has taught me a lot about asset design and modelling in 3d, even though I am still a beginner I can already make some nice looking assets.

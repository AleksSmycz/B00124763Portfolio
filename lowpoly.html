Why make low poly art

The low poly art pieces were made to show how a low poly art style would look in a game that I and a team of fiends had to make for a project. This evolved into creating several low poly pieces using adobe Illustrator.

Story behind the Low Poly art

I started off simple to get an idea of how difficult this would be I decided to create a cock in the low poly art style since they have a lot of colours this would show how easy it is to recognise without fine detail. I spent maybe an hour crating this first piece and I fell that it came out quite well. This was followed by the mountain top which I changed styles, instead of triangles I would now use quadrilaterals which would give the image a different feeling. This also came out well. For the last piece I decided to use a lot of triangles to show finer details this was time consuming process and took several hours.

All these pieces are similar yet distinct from one another showing just how versatile the art style can be.

Why make random vector designs

I made these random vector designs to practice using illustrator, and taken them from college projects in the past. It is a random assortment of projects I made for fun and actual work.

Story behind the vector designs

The vector designs where made using illustrator for practice of different style. The infinite triangles where created as a result of experimenting with logos and creating patterns, the neon colours give it a retro vibe. The barber shop logo was a result of me trying to create a logo for a fake barber shop as practice. The colours are from old barber shop poles.

The Skull is one of the first vector images I made. I created it from tracing over real skulls and it was illustrator practice however it turned out very well. Lastly the philosopher Plato. This was created for another college project but the vector design was not the finished product but was still very well made .
